Privacy

Security
We use Bungie's officially supported OAuth to verify you as a user. You do not need to create an account, and we do not ask for your credentials. We do not have access to your username, password, or email through Bungie. In order you use this app, you must approve access to this app through Bungie's website. In doing so, we get an access token that let's us communicate with Bungie's servers on your behalf.

You can revoke these permissions at any time here.

Every transmission is secured using HTTPS/SSL.

Information Collection & Storage
We do not try to store any personally identifying information. We may log your IP address to help fight spam.

Cookies
This app uses HTML5 local storage instead of cookies. Third party ad providers may store cookies on your device.
